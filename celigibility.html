Candidate Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Must be a currently enrolled student of the college.
  • Minimum CGPA of 7.0 or above.
  • No disciplinary actions in the academic record.
  • Active participation in college activities.
  • Must submit a nomination form before the deadline.

For more details, contact the election committee.
